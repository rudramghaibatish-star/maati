About Maati

Maati is a platform built to empower rural women artisans. We help them sell their crafts online, ensure fair pricing, and provide financial education through transparent trust schemes.

Founded with the belief that dignity and independence should be accessible to every artisan, Maati blends tradition with technology to create lasting impact.
